Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

come on you know what the fuck it is, mama mia! i cooka da pasta

you pretty much just cook some vegetables and add tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 package sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 package chopped mushrooms
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 large can crushed tomatoes
  • pasta of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat up a saucepan on the stove and add olive oil.
  2. Add the mushrooms and onions, stir for a few minutes until onions become translucent.
  3. Add the garlic and stir for about another minute, before it burns.
  4. Add in the cans of tomatoes, and stir it all up. Maybe throw in some italian seasoning.
  5. While the sauce cooks, boil some water and roll your sausage into little meatballs.
  6. Boil the meatballs until cooked all the way through, then add them to the sauce.
  7. Use the meatball water to boil your pasta.
