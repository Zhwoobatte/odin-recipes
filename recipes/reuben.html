Reuben

Sandwich! It's pretty simple and pretty good, you only need like five ingredients to make it.

The sauerkraut and corned beef combine really well together.

Ingredients

  • Rye bread
  • Corned beef
  • Sauerkraut
  • Swiss cheese
  • Thousand island dressing

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread, and spread the dressing on both sides.
  2. Layer three or four slices of corned beef on one slice evenly.
  3. Add the sauerkraut on top of the beef, then put a few slices of swiss cheese on the other side of the bread.
  4. Toast the sandwich in the oven to your desired crispiness.
  5. Once toasted, fold the melted cheese side on top of the other, then cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Eat the sandwich!
